This image helps in understanding super-entropic black holes and allows a more approachable discussion when talking about the **critical black holes** studied in [our paper](https://inspirehep.net/literature/1767690). It is interesting to see that the conical deficit on one extreme makes the black hole look like a needle for large (yet allowed) values of the acceleration or rotation.
