About me

I am a mexican Data Scientist with a PhD in Physics and experience in analysing and forecasting the behaviour of complex systems whilst sharing results with a wide range of audiences, from stakeholders to technicians via interactive, meaningful and dynamical dashboards.

Physics

For most of my adult life I've wondered about the meaning of things and the fundamental reasons about why the things that surround us behave the way they do.
